Tableau

        Bloom

        One seed grows into today's shape. Find which cell.

        How to play. Tap any open cell to plant a seed. Each turn it spreads into adjacent empty cells; walls block growth. After five turns, the bloom is checked against the faint target. Find the seed that grows into it exactly.
