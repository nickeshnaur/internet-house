Bounce

Four mirrors hide on a six-by-six grid. Five shots.

How to play. In Fire mode, tap a port — the beam enters, bounces off ╱ and ╲ mirrors at 45°, and exits somewhere. Only entry and exit glow; the path stays hidden. After your shots, switch to Place mode, drop four mirrors on the grid, and lock in.
